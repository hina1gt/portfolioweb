Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What is the purpose of this portfolio website?

A: This portfolio website serves as a platform for users to showcase their professional work and share insights through blog posts.


Q: How can I create a blog post on my portfolio?

A: To create a blog post, at first sign up or login. Then navigate to profile page simply by clicking 'Account' text on navbar. Lastly, click on button 'Add post' and create a post.


Q: Can I add another account?

A: Yes, and you can easily switch between them just by logging and loggingout.
